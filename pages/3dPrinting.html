HOW TO PRINT?

UPLOAD A PRINT

1. Find Your Model

Design your idea from scratch with a 3D modeling program of your choice or find a model online from Thingiverse or Printables.

2. Select Your Print Settings

Choose your detail, infill, and scale settings in Prusa Slicer.

3. Slice Your Print

Finalize your print settings by clicking the "slice" button. Then, input the time and weight settings when uploading your print.

4. Upload Your Print

Select your filament type and color and input any special settings and notes along with your STL file.

5. Pick Up Your Print

Wait for your print to be completed! You will receive emails when your print has been approved, when it begins printing, an when it is completed.

DOES MY PRINT NEED SUPPORTS

YOUR PRINT WILL NEED SUPPORTS IF...

  • Your model has intricate shapes
  • If there are voids inside a solid model.
  • your model has overhang larger than 45 degrees from the vertical plane.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you have Lightroom on your computers?
Due to the way Adobe licenses Lightroom, we are not able to provide it on our computers. However, we do offer Lightroom Classic.
What 3D modeling programs are available?
We currently have Blender and Maya installed on our computers. We also recommend Tinkercad, which is a browser based program.
Can I request to have a program installed on the computers?
Yes, you absolutely can request to install a program. There might be complications if it requires a license, if it is not compatible with Mac computers, or if it is not required for a course.
