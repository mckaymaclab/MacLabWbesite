What is it?

A Cricut is an electronic cutting machine that can cut all sorts of designs from materials like paper, vinyl, card stock, and iron-on transfers. Some Cricut machines can even cut leather and wood!

You can use the Cricut to cut just about anything you would typically cut with scissors or an X-acto knife. A Cricut can cut with much more precision and speed than would ever be achievable by hand!

FREQUENT QUESTION

How long the VR appointment is?
The appointments will usually be for an hour, but for study session we can create longer appointments if needed!
Do I need any experience to use the VR Lab?
You don’t! Come and have fun with us. For first time users we will train you with our First Step App until you feel confident with it!
What if I want to use the VR with more friends?
You are able to reserve a Study Room in the library and take the VR there. You can also cast the screen of the VR so you and your friends can have a fun time together!
