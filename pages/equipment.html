FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Am I able to renew my checkout over the phone?
In order to renew your checkout you will need to bring the equipment to the lab so we can inspect it.
For how long can I check out equipment?
Before checking out an equipment ask a Mac Lab employee how long you can keep it for as overdue equipment has a fee of $10 dollars per late day.
Do I have to pay to check out equipment?
No. All equipment is completely free for students.
Do you check out laptops for students
No, we only checkout laptops to Library Faculty.
