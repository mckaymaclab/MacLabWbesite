FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What materials are compatible with your Cricut?
With the Cricut Explore Air 2 you can cut paper, cardstock, vinyl, iron on, and fabric. See the Cricut Explore Air 2 page on the Cricut website for more details.
Am I able to use the Cricut in the Mac Lab without checking it out?
Yes! If you would like you can use the Cricut machine in the Mac Lab. However, you will still need to bring your own materials.
Are there materials I can buy from the Library for the Cricut?
No. Unfortunately you will need to purchase your own materials. The local Walmart has Cricut supplies as well as Amazon and the Cricut online store.
