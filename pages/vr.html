FREQUENTLY QUESTION

Can I use the VR without making an appointment?
Yes! You can use the VR without creating an appointment as long as no one is scheduled for that time.
Can I stay longer than my scheduled time?
Yes! As long as no one has scheduled the VR for the time slot right after you, you can use the VR as long as you want.
Can I bring friends with me to use the VR?
Yes! You can bring your friends and take turns using our VR headset, we even have games like “Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes” that you can play as a group.
Am I able to screen cast while playing VR?
Yes! Just ask one of the employees, and we can help you screen cast on one of our devices.
